Welcome to Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Teaching Hospital


Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Teaching Hospital is a teaching-oriented hospital (it was upgraded to teaching status in 2008) that serves as the teaching hospital of Gampaha Wickramarachchi Ayurveda Institute administered under the purview of Ministry of Health and Indigenous Medicine.
